# Protocol Abstraction Level vs Micromanagement

## The Idea
Review the codebase and AI rules to determine if we are getting too bogged down in details, thus violating the core concept of "bemyagent". 

Bemyagent should act as a protocol that leaves enough freedom to the human and their agent to define *how* to work together. Getting too detailed might also contradict the fundamental goal of optimizing token consumption.

## Goal
Evaluate the current rules and templates. Are they too restrictive? Are we micromanaging the AI instead of providing a broad framework? We need to ensure the protocol remains a lightweight scaffold rather than a heavy, token-consuming straitjacket.
